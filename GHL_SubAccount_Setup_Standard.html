Sub-Account Setup Standard

BMM's Standard Operating Procedure for GoHighLevel Client Accounts

Objective: Standardize all GHL sub-account configurations for consistent operations, accurate reporting, and scalable client management.

00 / Prerequisites

Before creating the sub-account, gather:

Client Information

  • Business Name & Timezone
  • Primary Contact Email & Phone
  • Website URL & Industry

Assets Needed

  • Logo (PNG, transparent, min 500px)
  • Brand colors (hex codes)
  • Business address & Social URLs

01 / Pipeline Setup

Sales Pipeline (8 Stages)

Path: Opportunities > Pipelines > + Add Pipeline

Stage Order Purpose
New Lead 1 Fresh inquiry, not yet contacted
Contacted 2 First contact attempted
Qualified 3 Meets criteria, potential fit
Appointment Booked 4 Discovery call scheduled
Proposal Sent 5 Quote/proposal delivered
Negotiation 6 Active discussions
Closed Won 7 Deal signed ✅
Closed Lost 8 Deal lost ❌

Onboarding Pipeline (Optional)

Stage Purpose
Kickoff Scheduled Onboarding call set
Assets Received Logos, access, content
Setup In Progress Building campaigns
Client Review Awaiting approval
Live Campaigns active ✅

03 / Tag Strategy

Naming Convention

Format: [category]_[value]

Category Examples Use For
source_ source_google, source_meta Traffic source
service_ service_ads, service_seo Service interest
status_ status_hot, status_warm Lead temperature
campaign_ campaign_spring2026 Campaign tracking

04 / Standard Workflows

Create these 8 essential workflows:

# Workflow Name Trigger Key Actions
1 Lead Response Form Submitted Instant SMS + Email
2 Lead Nurture Tag: nurture_active 5-day drip
3 Appointment Reminder Appt Booked 24hr + 1hr SMS
4 No-Show Follow-Up Appt No Show Reschedule outreach
5 Review Request Pipeline → Closed Won 7-day delay, request
6 Stale Lead Reactivation 30 days inactive Re-engagement
7 ROAS - Google Contact Created Add to Google Ads
8 ROAS - Meta Contact Created Facebook CAPI
⚠️ Critical: Ensure all SMS templates include opt-out language: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe."

05 / Calendar & Booking

Path: Calendars > + Add Calendar

  • Name: Discovery Call
  • Duration: 30 minutes
  • Buffer: 15 minutes
  • Reminders: 24hr + 1hr SMS

06 / Email & SMS Templates

Path: Marketing > Templates

Template Name Type Purpose
Welcome Email Email After form submission
Appointment Confirmation SMS After booking
Appointment Reminder SMS 24hr/1hr before
Review Request SMS After Closed Won

07 / Reputation Management

Path: Reputation > Settings

  • Configure Google review link
  • Activate Review Request workflow (#5)
  • Connect GMB for review monitoring (optional)

08 / Reporting Dashboard

Path: Dashboard > Widgets

Widget Metric
Pipeline Value Total opportunity value by stage
Leads This Month New contacts count
Appointments Booked Calendar bookings
Conversion Rate Lead → Closed Won %
Revenue Closed Won total value

09 / White-Label Settings

Sub-Account Branding

Path: Settings > Business Profile

  • Business name & logo uploaded
  • Brand colors applied
  • Timezone set correctly

Client Portal Access

Path: Settings > Team Management

  • Create client user (if applicable)
  • Set appropriate permissions
